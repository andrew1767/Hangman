Welcome to Hangman

Welcome to Hangman! The computer will think of a word and you will try to guess what it is one letter at a time. The number of dashes are the number of letters in the secret word. Guessing a correct letter from below will replaces those dashes for every letter contained in the secret word. If the letter guessed is incorrect, the computer will draw a segment of the gallows and the man will be closer to execution. The number of incorrect guesses before the game ends will be 10 guesses. That's it, have fun!
